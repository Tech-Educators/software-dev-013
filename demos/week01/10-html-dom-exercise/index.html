About Quokkas

Quokkas are small marsupials native to Australia. Known for their adorable faces and constant "smile," they are often referred to as the happiest animals on Earth. Quokkas are found mainly on Rottnest Island and enjoy a relatively predator-free environment.

Fun Facts about Quokkas

  • Quokkas are nocturnal, meaning they are most active at night.
  • They have a lifespan of about 10 years in the wild.
  • Quokkas can climb small trees and shrubs to find food.
  • Despite their small size, quokkas are herbivores and eat leaves, grass, and plants.
  • Their natural habitat includes swamps, forests, and scrublands.
